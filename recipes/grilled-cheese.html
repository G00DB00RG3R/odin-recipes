Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese (sometimes known as a toasted sandwich or cheese toastie) is a hot cheese sandwich typically prepared by heating slices of cheese between slices of bread with a cooking fat such as butter or mayonnaise on a frying pan, griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp of softened butter
  • 4 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar

Instructions

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon butter on one side of each slice of bread. With butter side down, top each slice of bread with about 1/2 cup of cheddar.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon of butter. Add two slices of bread, butter side down. Cook until bread is golden and cheese is starting to melt, about 2 minutes. Flip one piece of bread on top of the other and continue to cook until cheese is melty, about 30 seconds more.
  3. Repeat for the second sandwich, wiping skillet if necessary.
